Cultural Tourism

Discover the traditions, history, and heritage of the UAE.

About Cultural Tourism

Cultural tourism in the UAE gives visitors the opportunity to experience the nation’s heritage, architecture, museums, customs, and local traditions. It is an important way to understand the history and identity of the country.

Top Attractions

Louvre Abu Dhabi

A famous museum that combines art, culture, and history.

Al Fahidi Historical District

A heritage area that shows old Dubai architecture and culture.

Qasr Al Hosn

A historic fort and one of the oldest landmarks in Abu Dhabi.

Things to Do

Visitors can explore museums, visit heritage villages, learn about Emirati traditions, watch cultural performances, and enjoy historical landmarks. Tourists can also walk through traditional markets called souks, where they can see local crafts, spices, textiles, and souvenirs. Many cultural areas offer guided tours that explain the history of the buildings and the lifestyle of the Emirati people in the past. Visitors can try traditional Emirati food, watch local art exhibitions, and participate in cultural festivals that celebrate music, dance, and storytelling. Photography is also popular in historical districts because of the unique architecture and beautiful cultural settings.

Travel Tips

Dress respectfully when visiting heritage and cultural sites, especially in religious or traditional areas. Visitors should bring water and wear comfortable shoes because many cultural locations involve walking through museums, historic districts, and outdoor heritage villages. It is also recommended to visit during the cooler months, usually from November to March, when the weather in the UAE is more pleasant. Tourists should respect local customs and traditions, ask permission before taking photos of people, and follow the rules at cultural and historical sites. Bringing a camera, sunscreen, and a hat can also help make the visit more enjoyable.
