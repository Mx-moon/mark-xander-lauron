Entertainment Tourism

Enjoy fun attractions, theme parks, shows, and exciting entertainment experiences in the UAE.

About Entertainment Tourism

Entertainment tourism in the UAE offers visitors exciting attractions, theme parks, shopping festivals, and live performances. It is a perfect tourism type for families, friends, and tourists who want fun and memorable experiences.

Top Entertainment Attractions

Ferrari World

A famous theme park in Abu Dhabi known for thrilling rides and the world’s fastest roller coaster.

Motiongate Dubai

A movie-themed amusement park in Dubai featuring exciting rides, attractions, and family entertainment.

Global Village

A popular entertainment destination with shopping, live performances, cultural displays, and international food.

Things to Do

Visitors can enjoy thrilling rides, watch shows, attend festivals, explore theme parks, and try food and shopping activities.

Travel Tips

Check schedules and ticket prices before visiting. Weekdays are usually better if you want to avoid large crowds.
