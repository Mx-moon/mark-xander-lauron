Desert Tourism

Experience the beauty of golden sand dunes and exciting desert adventures in the UAE.

About Desert Tourism

Desert tourism in the United Arab Emirates allows visitors to explore the vast Arabian desert and experience unique cultural and adventure activities. Tourists can enjoy dune bashing, camel riding, sandboarding, and traditional desert camping. The desert landscapes offer breathtaking sunset views and unforgettable experiences under the stars.

Top Desert Attractions

Liwa Desert

Located in Abu Dhabi, Liwa Desert is famous for its massive sand dunes and beautiful desert scenery. It is a popular destination for desert safaris and cultural experiences.

Lahbab Desert

Lahbab Desert is known for its red sand dunes and exciting activities like dune bashing, sandboarding, and desert photography.

Al Badayer Desert

A famous desert location in Sharjah known for off-road driving, desert safaris, and breathtaking sunset views across the dunes.

Things to Do

Visitors can ride camels across the sand dunes, experience thrilling dune bashing in 4x4 vehicles, try sandboarding, and enjoy traditional desert camps with Arabic food and cultural performances. Watching the desert sunset and stargazing at night are also unforgettable experiences.

Travel Tips

Wear light and comfortable clothing and bring sunglasses, sunscreen, and plenty of water when visiting the desert. It is best to visit in the late afternoon or evening to avoid the extreme heat. Always join guided desert tours for safety when participating in adventure activities.
