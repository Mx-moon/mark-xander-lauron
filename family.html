Family Tourism

Enjoy fun attractions, theme parks, and family-friendly activities in the UAE.

About Family Tourism

Family tourism in the United Arab Emirates offers many attractions suitable for visitors of all ages. Families can enjoy theme parks, aquariums, shopping malls, interactive museums, and exciting entertainment centers designed for both children and adults.

Top Family Attractions

Ferrari World Abu Dhabi

A famous indoor theme park featuring exciting rides and attractions for the whole family.

Aquaventure Waterpark

A large water park in Dubai with slides, pools, and fun water activities for families.

Dubai Aquarium

A huge aquarium inside Dubai Mall where families can see sharks, fish, and marine life.

Things to Do

Families can enjoy theme parks, water parks, indoor attractions, aquariums, shopping malls, and fun activities suitable for children and adults across the UAE.

Travel Tips

Plan your visits early and check attraction schedules. Bring comfortable clothing and water, especially when traveling with children in warm weather.
