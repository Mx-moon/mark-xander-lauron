Nature Tourism

Experience mountains, lakes, mangroves, and natural beauty in the UAE.

About Nature Tourism

Nature tourism in the UAE allows visitors to enjoy the country’s natural attractions such as mountains, lakes, parks, and mangrove forests. It offers peaceful surroundings and outdoor activities that are perfect for relaxation and exploration.

Top Nature Attractions

Hatta Dam

A scenic destination surrounded by mountains where visitors can enjoy kayaking and peaceful natural views.

Mangrove National Park

A protected natural area in Abu Dhabi known for kayaking, wildlife, and beautiful mangrove forests.

Jebel Jais

The highest mountain in the UAE offering scenic roads, cool weather, and amazing viewpoints.

Location of Nature Attractions

Hatta Dam

Things to Do

Visitors can hike in the mountains, kayak through mangrove forests, explore nature parks, observe wildlife, take scenic photographs, and enjoy outdoor adventures in the peaceful natural areas of the UAE. Many destinations also offer guided eco-tours that help visitors learn more about the environment and local ecosystems.

Travel Tips

Wear comfortable outdoor clothing and bring water when visiting natural areas. Sunscreen and hats are recommended because of the warm weather. Always respect wildlife and follow environmental guidelines to help protect the natural beauty of the UAE.
