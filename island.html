Island Tourism

Discover beautiful islands, luxury resorts, and scenic coastal attractions in the UAE.

About Island Tourism

Island tourism in the UAE offers visitors a chance to enjoy luxury resorts, beaches, nature, and entertainment on some of the country’s most famous islands. These islands provide relaxing views, exciting activities, and unforgettable travel experiences for tourists.

Top Island Attractions

Palm Jumeirah

A world-famous man-made island in Dubai known for luxury hotels, private beaches, and modern attractions.

Yas Island

A popular island in Abu Dhabi that offers theme parks, shopping, entertainment, and family attractions.

Sir Bani Yas Island

A natural island destination known for wildlife, eco tourism, and peaceful luxury resorts.

Things to Do

Visitors can stay in island resorts, enjoy beach activities, visit theme parks, relax by the sea, and explore scenic island attractions.

Travel Tips

Bring comfortable clothes, sunscreen, and a camera. Book your island resort or attraction tickets early during busy seasons.
