Beach Tourism

Enjoy the beautiful beaches and relaxing coastal views of the UAE.

About Beach Tourism

Beach tourism in the United Arab Emirates attracts visitors who want to relax by the sea and enjoy warm weather. The UAE has many beautiful beaches where tourists can swim, enjoy water sports, and experience luxury beach resorts with amazing ocean views.

Top Beach Attractions

JBR Beach

A famous beach in Dubai known for restaurants, shopping areas, and water activities.

Kite Beach

A popular destination for kite surfing, jogging paths, and relaxing seaside views.

Saadiyat Beach

A beautiful beach in Abu Dhabi with white sand and luxury resorts.

Location of Beach Attractions

JBR Beach

Kite Beach

Saadiyat Beach

Things to Do

Visitors can swim, relax on the beach, try jet skiing, parasailing, and enjoy seaside restaurants while watching beautiful sunsets.

Travel Tips

Bring sunscreen, sunglasses, and comfortable beachwear. Visit during the morning or late afternoon to avoid strong sunlight.
