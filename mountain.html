Mountain Tourism

Discover scenic mountain landscapes, cool weather, and outdoor adventures in the UAE.

About Mountain Tourism

Mountain tourism in the United Arab Emirates offers visitors a different side of the country beyond deserts and city skylines. The UAE has beautiful mountain areas that provide cooler weather, breathtaking views, and exciting outdoor activities. These places are perfect for travelers who enjoy hiking, sightseeing, camping, and exploring natural landscapes. Mountain tourism also allows visitors to experience peaceful surroundings and traditional communities near the highland areas.

Top Mountain Attractions

Jebel Jais

The highest mountain in the UAE, famous for its spectacular views, cool climate, and thrilling zipline attraction.

Hatta

A mountain destination known for outdoor adventures, kayaking, hiking trails, and its peaceful natural environment.

Jebel Hafeet

A well-known mountain in Al Ain offering winding roads, scenic lookouts, and panoramic views of the surrounding area.

Things to Do

Visitors can hike through mountain trails, enjoy camping, take photographs of beautiful views, drive along scenic roads, and try adventure activities such as ziplining and kayaking in nearby areas. Mountain tourism in the UAE is ideal for people who want a relaxing but active travel experience surrounded by nature and fresh air.

Travel Tips

Wear comfortable shoes and bring enough water when visiting mountain areas. The weather may be cooler than in the city, so it is good to bring a light jacket, especially in the early morning or evening. Check road and weather conditions before traveling, and always follow safety rules if you plan to hike or try adventure activities in the mountains.
